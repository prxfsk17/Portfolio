What is Formula 1?

Formula 1 is the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars. It's a global spectacle that combines cutting-edge technology, incredible human skill, and pure adrenaline.

Speed

F1 cars are the fastest racing machines on the planet, reaching speeds of over 230 mph (370 km/h). The acceleration and braking forces push drivers to their physical limits.

Technology

The pinnacle of automotive engineering. Hybrid power units, advanced aerodynamics, and data analytics make F1 a moving laboratory for future road car technologies.

Drama

Rivalries, controversies, and unexpected victories. Every race weekend tells a unique story of human triumph and heartbreak at 200 miles per hour.
